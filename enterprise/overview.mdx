NodePad Enterprise is designed for organizations that cannot send data to third-party infrastructure, operate in regulated industries, or have compliance requirements that a standard SaaS product cannot meet. It includes everything in the public beta, plus the controls you need to run NodePad entirely on your own terms — in your VPC, behind your firewall, with your identity provider and your model endpoints.

<Note>
  NodePad Enterprise is separate from the public beta. The beta runs on NodePad-managed cloud infrastructure. Enterprise deployments run on **your** infrastructure, under **your** control.
</Note>

## Who Enterprise is for

NodePad Enterprise is built for teams where data sovereignty is non-negotiable:

- **Regulated industries** — healthcare, finance, legal, and government organizations subject to HIPAA, SOC 2, FedRAMP, or similar frameworks
- **Defense and intelligence** — teams operating in classified or air-gapped environments
- **Enterprises with existing identity infrastructure** — organizations that require SSO and automated user lifecycle management
- **Teams with custom model requirements** — organizations running their own inference endpoints via Ollama, vLLM, Amazon Bedrock, or Google Vertex AI

## Your data stays yours

NodePad never trains on your conversations. On the Enterprise plan, your data never leaves your infrastructure at all — it stays inside the environment you control, subject to the policies you set.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What about the beta — is my data safe there?">
    Yes. Even on the public beta, NodePad does not train models on your conversations. The beta runs on NodePad-managed cloud infrastructure, so data is processed on NodePad's servers. Enterprise deployments eliminate this entirely — your data never leaves your network.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I bring my own model API keys?">
    Yes. Enterprise lets you connect your own keys for external model providers, or point NodePad at your own inference endpoints running Ollama, vLLM, Amazon Bedrock, or Google Vertex AI.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is air-gapped deployment really supported?">
    Yes. NodePad is designed from the ground up to support fully air-gapped deployments. No outbound calls are required once the software is installed in your environment.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
